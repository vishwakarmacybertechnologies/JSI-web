Corporate Heritage

Forging the backbone of India's industrial growth since 2008.

A Legacy of Excellence

Established in 2008, Jaydeep Steel (India) operates at the nexus of India's industrial transformation. Headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we function as a critical supply node for the heavy machinery, pharmaceutical, power generation, and construction sectors.

As premier stockists and suppliers of Ferrous and Non-Ferrous Metals, our procurement and distribution networks are built to handle massive bulk orders. We ensure that vital infrastructure projects are never halted by material shortages, delivering uncompromising quality on every order.

Vision & Mission

The driving forces behind our global supply operations.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and universally recognized supplier of industrial raw materials in India, setting the global benchmark for material integrity, supply chain efficiency, and corporate reliability.

Our Mission

To empower massive infrastructural growth by delivering precision-engineered steel, vital components, and unwavering support to refineries, plants, and construction projects worldwide.

Strategic Leadership

The scale of our operations requires meticulous oversight and unyielding dedication to client satisfaction. Under the strategic direction of our Management Head, Dinesh Rao, Jaydeep Steel (India) has cultivated a reputation for executing complex supply chain logistics with flawless precision.

Our internal quality policy mandates that every pipe, flange, and valve we supply is rigorously tested, strictly adhering to NACE, ASTM, BS, IS, ASA, and DIN international standards.

National Commitments

We are dedicated to the progress and sustainability of our nation.

Make in India

We actively empower domestic manufacturing by ensuring a steady, high-quality flow of raw materials to Indian factories, reinforcing the nation's self-reliance in heavy infrastructure and production.

Swachh Bharat

We are deeply committed to clean industrial practices. Our storage, handling, and logistics operations are optimized to minimize environmental impact and maintain immaculate operational facilities.

Long-term Partnerships

We believe in building the nation by building relationships. We partner with the most critical sectors—from pharmaceuticals to energy—to ensure they have the resources to succeed.
